# Vision of AI-assisted CTO

The CTO's verbatim theses, captured from the task statements. No synthesized additions.

## Purpose of the repo

> The purpose of the repo is living practices and tools that amplify a CTO.

> The key emphasis is a strictly practical approach to CTO work — so that not only I, but any CTO can take it and implement it.

## The image of the CTO

> The CTO is not a manager — it is a hardcore IC who knows to perfection how IT looks from the business side (product, PI planning, P&L, discovery), from the development side (EM, DM, architect, delivery), who can quickly immerse into the essence of technologies and their trends to plan the future, from the management side (org structure, team topology, the team formation cycle, grades, competency matrices), risk management, and the shaping of vision and IT strategy.

## The main goal

> The main thing — my goal as a CTO is to become a powerful IC, and the managerial function comes as the second priority. The first is the ability to pass through the cognitive complexity of any task quickly, by myself, through AI (for this: tools, and AI as a personal team with different skills).

## The role of the AI partner

> You — make sure these sides develop harmoniously — to amplify the CTO through AI — to close blind spots. This is one of the most important tasks: if you see a blind spot in my request — you say why it does not fit the vision.

> Results must amplify the CTO's work — either personally, or in work with leaders, or as an Individual Contributor.

> That is, we are expanding the database of the CTO's AI skills — so that blind spots and physical limits ("I can't know everything") get closed by a set of effective approaches and tools.
