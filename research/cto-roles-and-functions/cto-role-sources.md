# Sources — CTO roles and functions

The annotated source base behind the [role tree](cto-role-tree.md), merged from all research lenses and deduplicated. Every link verified live on 2026-07-23; labels: primary source / retelling. Grouped by the function map.

## The role itself

- **[The Engineering Executive's Primer — Will Larson](https://lethain.com/eng-execs-primer/)** — the operating manual for the CTO seat (24 chapters, drafts free on the blog); the single most load-bearing source of this research. Primary.
- **[On the role of CTO — Camille Fournier](https://www.elidedbranches.com/2015/02/cto.html)** — "executive first, technologist second"; the definitional essay. Primary.
- **[The Manager's Path, ch. "The Big Leagues" — Fournier](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/the-managers-path/9781491973882/ch08.html)** — VP Eng vs CTO, senior-leadership models, delivering bad news. Primary.
- **[Your first 90 days as CTO or VPE — Larson](https://lethain.com/first-ninety-days-cto-vpe/)** — the entry playbook; a shared definition of success with the CEO. Primary.
- **[The Different CTO Roles — Werner Vogels](https://www.allthingsdistributed.com/2007/07/the_different_cto_roles.html)** — four CTO archetypes; a lens on weighting the function map. Primary.
- **[9 things every CEO needs their CTO to know — CTO Craft](https://ctocraft.com/blog/9-things-every-ceo-needs-their-cto-to-know/)** — CEO-expectations list; companion: [how CTOs succeed at the leaders' table](https://ctocraft.com/blog/welcome-to-the-c-suite-how-ctos-succeed-at-the-leaders-table/); the main CTO community. Primary/community.
- **[CTO Academy — Digital MBA curriculum](https://cto.academy/the-digital-mba-for-technology-leaders/)** — a 9-module function taxonomy of the role. Primary (curriculum).
- **[Startup CTO Handbook — Zach Goldberg](https://github.com/ZachGoldberg/Startup-CTO-Handbook)** — free full book; people/technical-management/architecture taxonomy for heads of engineering. Primary.
- **[awesome-cto — kuchin](https://github.com/kuchin/awesome-cto)** — curated index incl. due-diligence and finance sections. Curated list of retellings.

## Strategy and architecture

- **[Writing an engineering strategy — Larson](https://lethain.com/eng-strategies/)** — diagnosis → guiding policies → coherent actions; the strategy format every audience lens converged on. Primary.
- **[Wardley Maps — Simon Wardley](https://medium.com/wardleymaps)** ([on-ramp](https://learnwardleymapping.com/)) — evolution-based build-vs-buy and strategy narratives. Primary.
- **[Build versus buy — Larson](https://lethain.com/build-vs-buy/)** — vendor valuation logic, quarterly scorecards. Primary.
- **[Documenting Architecture Decisions — Michael Nygard](https://cognitect.com/blog/2011/11/15/documenting-architecture-decisions)** — the original ADR format; [templates collection](https://github.com/joelparkerhenderson/architecture-decision-record). Primary.
- **[Build Your Own Radar — Thoughtworks](https://www.thoughtworks.com/radar/byor)** — method and tooling for an internal tech radar. Primary.

## Org design

- **[Team Topologies — key concepts](https://teamtopologies.com/key-concepts)** — team types, interaction modes, cognitive load; [Team API template](https://github.com/TeamTopologies/Team-API-template). Primary.
- **[Sizing engineering teams — Larson](https://lethain.com/sizing-engineering-teams/)** — 6–8 per manager, grow-then-bud. Primary.
- **[An Elegant Puzzle — Larson](https://lethain.com/elegant-puzzle/)** — org sizing, reorgs, succession planning. Primary.

## People and talent

- **[Dropbox Engineering Career Framework](https://dropbox.github.io/dbx-career-framework/)** — complete real-world ladder incl. QA/SRE tracks; borrowable wholesale. Primary.
- **[progression.fyi](https://progression.fyi/)** — 75 public career frameworks for benchmarking. Aggregator of primary artifacts.
- **[levels.fyi](https://www.levels.fyi/)** — market comp benchmarking per level. Primary (market data).
- **[StaffEng guides — Larson](https://staffeng.com/guides/)** — staff-plus archetypes, promotion packets, org placement. Primary.
- **[The Engineer/Manager Pendulum — Charity Majors](https://charity.wtf/2017/05/11/the-engineer-manager-pendulum/)** — management is a profession change, not a promotion; technical credibility. Primary.
- **[What does sponsorship look like? — Lara Hogan](https://larahogan.me/blog/what-sponsorship-looks-like/)** — concrete sponsorship acts for leads; companion: [Manager Voltron](https://larahogan.me/blog/manager-voltron/). Primary.
- **[Career levels and more — Larson](https://lethain.com/career-levels-and-more/)** — designing levels and managing designation momentum. Primary.
- **[Zero regrettable attrition is the wrong target — First Round](https://review.firstround.com/why-this-engineering-leader-thinks-you-shouldnt-aim-for-zero-regrettable-attrition/)** — retention targets from Greenhouse's CTO. Retelling (edited interview).

## Delivery and engineering process

- **[How to plan as an engineering executive — Larson](https://lethain.com/planning/)** — the three-layer cadence (financial plan → allocation → roadmap); anti-thrash. Primary.
- **[Measuring an engineering organization — Larson](https://lethain.com/measuring-engineering-organizations/)** — audience-split metrics; what boards should and shouldn't see. Primary.
- **[DORA](https://dora.dev/)** — four keys + [capabilities catalog](https://dora.dev/capabilities/) as the improvement backlog. Primary (research).
- **[The SPACE of Developer Productivity](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/publication/the-space-of-developer-productivity-theres-more-to-it-than-you-think/)** — productivity is multidimensional; anti-velocity-chart armor. Primary.
- **[DevEx: What Actually Drives Productivity — Noda, Storey, Forsgren, Greiler](https://getdx.com/report/devex-productivity/)** — feedback loops, cognitive load, flow; the survey model. Primary.
- **[Scaling engineering teams via RFCs — Gergely Orosz](https://blog.pragmaticengineer.com/scaling-engineering-teams-via-writing-things-down-rfcs/)** — the lightweight RFC process. Primary (practitioner).
- **[Maker's Schedule, Manager's Schedule — Paul Graham](https://www.paulgraham.com/makersschedule.html)** — the canonical essay on the cost of meetings for makers. Primary.
- **[Work the policy, not the exceptions — Larson](https://lethain.com/work-policy-not-exceptions/)** — escalations as policy inputs. Primary.
- **[Meetings for an effective eng organization — Larson](https://lethain.com/eng-org-meetings/)** — the full leadership meeting stack. Primary.
- **[ActionableAgile — Daniel Vacanti](https://actionableagile.com/)** — flow metrics and probabilistic forecasting over point estimates. Primary.

## Operations, reliability and security

- **[Postmortem Culture — Google SRE book](https://sre.google/sre-book/postmortem-culture/)** — blameless postmortems and leadership's role; [the SRE books](https://sre.google/books/). Primary.
- **[The SOC2 Starting Seven — Latacora](https://www.latacora.com/blog/2020/03/12/the-soc-starting/)** — the concrete pre-compliance security adoption order for SMBs. Primary.
- **[Incident communication best practices — incident.io](https://incident.io/blog/incident-communication-best-practices)** — stakeholder comms: severity-linked SLAs, comms lead, status page. Primary (practitioner).
